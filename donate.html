Support Us

Open defecation and poor sanitation is one of the most challenging social problems that we, as a country face today. We have the tool to fight the problem, and like every tool, it needs to keep growing and improving. The problem does not rest, nor do we. And for that, we need your support. We ask you to contribute to this cause by donating to our organization. Your donations would help us in research and development, capacity building, hiring, construction of toilets, and upscaling. All donations are income tax exempted.

Do not underestimate the power of your small contribution – we, as a united population, can bring a revolution and slay this problem once and for all. Let’s help each other!
