Our Story

Every tale has a challenge – a war between two sides. The war we are inviting you to join is against a powerful monster, but we are not the heroes of this tale.

We are the engineers who design the hero’s sword.

Sustainable, Contemporary and Renewable Initiatives for Transforming India, or SUKRITI is a not-for-profit company working in collaboration with the Indian Institute of Technology (IIT), Roorkee and University of Surrey, United Kingdom towards social and economic upliftment of the country through sustainable engineering initiatives and innovations.

Our core team comprises of engineering graduates from IIT Roorkee who have come together to develop technologies through a combination of expertise from various engineering disciplines.

In this narrative, we are the propellers – we are the cogs in the greater scheme of events. The heroes of this narrative are each and every one of the 1.25 billion people that live in India. Our goal is to develop technology to fight monsters of the modern world.

We, at SUKRITI Social Foundation, aim to change the present sanitary conditions, come up with a solid wastemanagement plan for a clean India, work towards energy sufficiency by improving the penetration of alternative andrenewable energy, develop products for better governance, work on low-cost housing and health-care, skill developmentand agricultural sustenance. Basically, we are a bunch of ambitious people who want to work towards solving some of themost pressing issues in the country, in a way that is economical and environment-friendly, through use of engineering solutions.

At present, we are working primarily in the Water, Sanitation and Hygiene (WASH) sector. We are committed to changing the current scenario of this sector and bringing change to the lives of millions who are still forced to defecate in the open.
